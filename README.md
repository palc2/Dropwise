# 💧 Dropwise

### *Every drop, tracked wisely*

A simple, private milk tracking app built for breastfeeding and pumping moms. No account needed, no data leaving your phone — everything lives right in your browser.

-----

## Getting Started

Open the HTML file in any browser on your phone or computer. That’s it — no install, no sign-up.

The app opens straight to today’s log. Your data is saved automatically as you type.

-----

## Features

### 📋 Daily Log

Each day shows your pumping sessions laid out in a list. For every session you can:

- **Log volume** — enter how much you pumped in oz or ml
- **Log duration** — track how long each session took in minutes
- **Mark done** — tap the coloured dot on the left to check a session off
- **Rename a session** — tap the session name (e.g. “Session 1”) to type your own label, like “after school pickup” or “before bed”
- **Edit the time** — tap the time (e.g. “6:00 am”) to change it to whatever fits your day
- **Reorder sessions** — press and hold the ⠿ grip on the left of any card and drag it up or down to rearrange

A summary bar at the top shows your **total volume**, **sessions completed**, **average per session**, and **last session** at a glance, updating live as you log.

You can also add a **day note** at the bottom — useful for flagging things like “low supply day” or “started fenugreek.”

-----

### ⏱ Sessions Per Day

Choose between **2 and 12 sessions per day** using the selector in the top-right corner. The app automatically builds an evenly spaced schedule across 24 hours starting at 6:00 am, with all times rounded to clean :00 or :30 slots.

Change the session count any time — your logged data is never lost.

-----

### ✏️ Write Your Own Schedule

Not happy with the default times? Tap **“Write your own schedule”** below your session list to open the schedule editor.

- Edit any time slot to match your real routine
- Drag and drop rows to reorder them
- Save once and the schedule applies to **all future days** — your past logged data is never affected
- Hit **Reset** to go back to the automatic even schedule any time

-----

### 📏 oz or ml

Switch between fluid ounces and millilitres using the **oz / ml** toggle in the top-right corner. All your stored data converts instantly — nothing is lost when you switch units.

-----

### 📊 Charts

Tap the **Charts** tab to see your last 14 days visualised.

**Daily total view** shows:

- 🔵 Blue bars — your total volume pumped each day
- 🟡 Amber line — your average volume per session that day, so you can see if individual sessions are improving even on lower-total days

**By session view** shows a stacked bar chart breaking down how much each session slot contributed to the day’s total.

Tap or hover any bar to see both values in a tooltip.

-----

### 📅 History

The **History** tab shows a clean table of the last 14 days with total volume, sessions completed, and any day notes you left. Good for spotting patterns at a glance or sharing a quick summary with a lactation consultant.

-----

### 💾 Your Data

Everything is stored **on your device only** — Dropwise never sends data anywhere.

In the **Data** tab you can:

- **Export JSON** — download a backup file of all your data
- **Import JSON** — load a backup back in, or sync between your phone and laptop by exporting on one device and importing on the other

Importing is smart about conflicts — if a session already has data on the current device, it keeps your existing entry rather than overwriting it.

-----

## Tips

- **Dragging sessions** on the log updates your schedule going forward, so tomorrow’s list will already reflect the new order.
- **Editing a session name or time** on the log saves it as your custom schedule for future days too — you only need to do it once.
- **The average per session line** on the chart is often more useful than the total — a consistent average means your supply is stable even on busy days when you miss a session.
- If you track on your phone and want to view charts on a bigger screen, just export on your phone and import on your laptop.

-----

## Privacy

Dropwise stores everything in your browser’s local storage. Nothing is uploaded, tracked, or shared. Clearing your browser data will erase your logs, which is why the Export feature exists.
